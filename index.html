Towers of Hanoi

How To Play:

Move all discs, from the first tower to the last tower, while stacking smaller discs only on larger discs.

Try to get as close to the minimum number of moves as possible in the shortest amount of time.

Click Reset after successfully completing a game or to abort a game in process.

Have fun!

Discs: 8
Minimum Moves: 255 Your Moves: 0
